Hi, my name is michael

I am a self-employed freelancer in the IT Industry and recently relocated to the coastal town of Swakopmund. In February of 2023, I fell into a Diabetic Coma for 12 days, leaving my Cerebellum without oxygen for 15 minutes. This has rendered me unable to walk or do any basic tasks for myself. I was subsequently hospitalized for a further 7 weeks and have only returned home at the end of April 2023. Due to the coma, I am unable to work for the next 10 months, as I have to undergo Physiotherapy, Occupational Therapy and Pshycological appointments. We have created a BackaBuddy campaign where we desperately ask for donations to cover Medical Expenses, Rehabilitation and day-to-day expenses I cannot cover due to being unable to work.

You can find the link to my BackaBuddy Campaign at the bottom of this Page
